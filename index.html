About The Story Tree

Nurturing growth through storytelling and training

Inception

The Story Tree is a Jaipur based company which focuses on creating value and honing the "Soft Skills" of Students and working Adults alike. The founder – Pallavi Singh has been a trainer and Story Teller for several years.

Vision

The Story Tree is created as a direct result of passion for nurturing via training individuals from across social strata's and defying age as a barrier for learning. At The Story Tree, we believe that life is a journey full of learning experiences and aimed at self and community growth!

Objective

The Story Tree workshop is designed to encourage participants towards:

  • Nurturing confidence through improved self-expression
  • Being better communicators, team players
  • Developing listening skills
  • Exploring, learning and healing through stories and discussions
